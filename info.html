About the Crew

History of Crew

The film crew of this horror movie initially had a low budget for this film of about $60,000. This film was shot in 8 full days by this film crew, which is impressive. However, one controversial action was the film crew possibly starving and sleep-depriving the actors/actresses for a more realistic acting. Also, 3 of the film crew members by the names of Heather donahue, Joshua Leonard, and Michael C. Williams were not very experienced in film, which makes the movie's production even more impressive.
